About VIN

VIN blends realistic traffic simulation with AI predictions and an operator‑first interface. It highlights hotspots, explains changes clearly, and keeps the experience fast and simple.

  • Live Ops: Monitor flow in real time and act fast.
  • AI Alerts: Early warnings for congestion and anomalies.
  • Analytics & Maps: Clear trends, clean charts, and city overlays.
  • Ready to Run: Dockerized for quick demos and deploys.

Key Features

Realtime

Live data collection with configurable refresh interval and status pill.

ML Prediction

Congestion prediction using scikit‑learn models and alerting rules.

Analytics

Smart detection of speed or volume across datasets; robust charts.

Map

City map with colored scatter layers by alert severity and tooltips.

Exports

CSV download for both live and processed datasets.

Dockerized

Ready‑to‑run container images for quick demos and deployments.
